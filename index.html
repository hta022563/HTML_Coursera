About Images

Facebook

Still the world's largest social media platform in terms of monthly active users. People use Facebook to connect with friends and family, join groups, follow pages and businesses, and share information and updates.


YouTube

Although often seen as a video platform, YouTube also has a strong community feature, allowing users to interact through commenting, liking, sharing, and posting their own content. With a large number of users searching for and watching videos every day, it is one of the most influential online platforms.


Instagram

Focusing on sharing photos and short videos, Instagram is especially popular with young people and those interested in lifestyle, travel, fashion and art. Features like Stories, Reels and IGTV also contribute to its popularity.
